Grand Opening of Nova Café in Kyiv

A New Spot for Coffee Lovers

The city of Kyiv is about to welcome a brand-new coffee shop, Nova Café, in the heart of Podil. The café, known for its locally sourced beans and cozy atmosphere, will open its doors this Saturday. Coffee enthusiasts are eager to try their special brews and homemade pastries.

"We wanted to create a place where people could enjoy high-quality coffee and feel at home," said Oksana Petrenko, the café's owner.

With its minimalist interior and warm lighting, Nova Café aims to become a favorite spot for remote workers and students. The menu will feature a variety of espresso-based drinks, alternative brewing methods, and a selection of desserts.

Special Opening Event

The grand opening event will include live music and a free tasting of their signature drinks. The first 50 visitors will receive a free coffee of their choice.
